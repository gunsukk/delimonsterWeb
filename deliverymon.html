Deliverymon

Deliveymon is an app that connects the delivery requests with a driver.
All drivers must be registered with Delimon before driving.
After confirmation, the driver can install Deliverymon and begin delivery.
The driver can manage their available hours; orders will be received during those hours.
The driver delivers the food to users using navigation provided on Deliverymon.
The delivery is complete after users sign when prompted on Deliverymon.
