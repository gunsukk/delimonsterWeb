About Delimonster

Who We Are

Delimonster Inc. provides a comprehensive solution through our app, ‘Delimon’. We offer comfortable and convenient delivery services, allowing customers to access various restaurants in their vicinity with just a few simple taps. Customers begin by searching for a restaurant that matches their palate. Once a restaurant is found, he/she can easily order and check out through a simple app called ‘Delimon’. Delimonster Inc has been developing Indoor GPS app related to Beacon and AI services since 2012. Delimonster Inc has developed 3 apps for food delivery service, one for customers called as ‘Delimon’, one for restaurants called as ‘Delicacy’ and one for delivery people called as ‘Deliverymon’.

Benefits & Social Media Marketing Service

Many restaurant owners try to to reduce their cost of operation in order to maximize profits. One way owners try to achieve this is by hiring less employees. Delimonster help social media marketing through Newspaper, Yelp, Facebook, Instagram and Twitter. Delimonster Inc. is working to support both restaurant owners, by increasing their total revenue, and the general public, by creating more jobs.Through Delimon, we are working to create employment opportunities for people who want to earn money on their own schedules or anyone else looking for some extra cash.
